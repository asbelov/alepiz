'''ALEPIZ'''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://alepiz.com/help/overview.pug|title=Description of ALEPIZ}}</ref> is an [[open-source software|open-source]] [[free software]] designed for managing [[Software as a service|SaaS]] environments for [[System monitor|monitoring]] and [[Systems_management|managing]] IT components. ALEPIZ allows to configure infrastructure monitoring and link monitoring events with management actions. As a result, actions will be performed automatically, depending on the monitoring events that have occurred. ALEPIZ contains most of the necessary components and adapters for monitoring and managing infrastructure. The modular architecture allows the development of additional extensions for collecting information for monitoring or performing control actions. ALEPIZ includes a database, a web server and does not require installation of additional software.

ALEPIZ is released under the [[GNU General Public License version 3]]. ALEPIZ is [[free software]].

==Features==
* Organization of monitoring
* Infrastructure management
* Creation of workflows in the organization
* Ability to develop your own modules
* Auto detection of IT components
* High performance
* Web interface
* Monitoring capabilities with or without agents
* Support for various protocols for collecting information
